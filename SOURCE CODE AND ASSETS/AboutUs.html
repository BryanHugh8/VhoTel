Story
In the late 1990s, creative entrepreneurs created a proposal that would eventually become VhoTel. With its first guests in 1999, it has redefined luxury with its first-rate lodgings, top-notch amenities, and attentive service.
History
VhoTel was founded in 1999 and soon rose to become one of the most prestigious 5-star luxury hotels in the world. Its founders, motivated by a vision of luxury and sophistication, spared no expense in producing an architectural masterpiece that expertly merged classic appeal with cutting-edge design.
